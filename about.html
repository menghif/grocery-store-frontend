About Us

Gastronomia Giovanni delivers artisanal Italian products right to your door. Our products are sourced from organic farmers and producers who value quality as well as sustainability.

We understand our responsibility to the environment and we off-set our carbon emissions (i.e. from air shipment) with green investments. We want to share our love for Italian cuisine without comprising our respect and love for the earth. We hope you enjoy our products as much as we do.
